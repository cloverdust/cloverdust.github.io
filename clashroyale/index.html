Clash Royale is a real-time strategy game developed by Supercell, the creators of Clash of Clans. It combines elements of collectible card games, tower defense, and multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) games. In the game players draw cards against each other in fast-paced, real-time battles. The objective is to destroy your opponent's towers while defending your own. Each match typically lasts around 3 to 4 minutes, making it ideal for quick gaming sessions. The key to success in Clash Royale lies in building a balanced deck, anticipating your opponent's moves, and making quick, strategic decisions during battles. Combining different types of cards (offensive, defensive, and support) can create powerful synergies and counter enemy strategies effectively. Fast Card Deployment with Custom Key Mapping

We’ve assigned the keys 1, 2, 3, and 4 so you can quickly select cards. Once a card is selected, all you need to do is click on the location where you want to deploy the troops.

However, there are situations where speed is more important than precise placement, like when rushing your opponent or mounting a counterattack while defending your side of the court. In such cases, the WASD movement keys come in handy, to start place this in the mid section of the court. Then all you have to do is: 

  • Press W to deploy troops to the farthest point.

  • To target the left Queen Tower, press A then W.

  • To target the right Queen Tower, press D then W.

This way, you don’t need to focus on exact troop placement and can concentrate on defending your side effectively.
